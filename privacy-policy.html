BuyByPi Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

BuyByPi collects your Pi Network username and wallet address when you authenticate with the Pi SDK. We also collect information about listings you create on the marketplace.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide the BuyByPi marketplace service, process Pi payments, and display your listings to other users.

3. Pi Network Data

BuyByPi integrates with the Pi Network SDK. Authentication and payment data is handled through Pi Network's secure infrastructure. We do not store your private keys or seed phrase.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your Pi username may be visible to other users when you post listings or complete transactions.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All transactions are secured through the Pi Network blockchain.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us through the BuyByPi app on Pi Browser.
